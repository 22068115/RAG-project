Data is ubiquitous in this digital age and plays an important role in all careers. As a data scientist, you are required to have the expertise to convert all forms of data into valuable information. This Bachelor of Data Science degree will provide you with the essential skills and knowledge in Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), experimental design, building and fitting models for analysis, visualisation, estimation and prediction, analysis of complex data relationships, and storage and retrieval of big data. These are instrumental skills needed in many roles including the analysis of customer transactions and behaviour, scientific investigations, financial trends and online behaviour.

Learning Outcomes
Collapse all
Upon completion of this program, graduates will be able to:
1.
Select appropriate techniques for extracting, modelling, predicting, visualising, and managing data in practical scenarios, drawing on statistics, mathematics, and computer science approaches.

keyboard_arrow_down
2.
Use data ethically and responsibly to provide information and advice that is reliable, valid, timely, innovative and relevant for their chosen specialty.

keyboard_arrow_down
3.
Communicate effectively to diverse audiences the findings of data analyses which integrates computational, statistical, and machine learning methods, programming tools, and critical evaluation to ensure integrity, validity and quality assurance.

keyboard_arrow_down
4.
Solve problems through the design and execution of data-driven projects, integrating disciplinary knowledge and technical skills to address real-world questions.

keyboard_arrow_down
5.
Collaborate effectively and inclusively in professional environments and interdisciplinary contexts, exercising ethical judgement and professional responsibility in the use of data and technology.

keyboard_arrow_down
6.
Apply adaptive thinking and other meta-cognitive strategies for continued professional development in data science.
